Catch Note - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

1. Overview

Catch Note ("we", "our", or "the app") is a real-time voice pitch detection application developed by CMORE. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

Microphone Access

  • The app requires microphone permission to detect voice pitch in real-time.
  • Audio data is processed locally on your device only.
  • We do NOT record, store, or transmit any audio data to external servers.
  • Audio processing happens in real-time and is immediately discarded after pitch detection.

Recording History

  • Pitch detection results (note names, frequencies) may be saved locally on your device.
  • This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

3. Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use data according to Google's privacy policy. For more information, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps

4. Data Security

Since all audio processing occurs locally on your device and no personal data is transmitted to our servers, your data remains secure on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
